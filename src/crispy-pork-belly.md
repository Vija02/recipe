Crispy pork belly
======

<h3>Initial Prep</h3>

1. Salt all sides
2. Wash
3. Boil 10m (Skin side down, water half way)

Seasoning 

    "Seasoning" = combine(
      0.5tsp five spice,
      0.33tsp black pepper,
      0.25tsp white pepper,
      0.5tsp salt,
      1tsp garlic salt
    )

<h3>Get it ready</h3>

    Add to top(Add to bottom(Score top and bottom(Pork belly), Seasoning), Vinegar, Salt)

<h2>Then: Oven 1h, 200c</h2>

Then just need to deep fry
